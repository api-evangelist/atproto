AT Protocol launched a new podcast, Off Protocol, that consolidates its livestreamed office hours Q&A with longer-form interviews featuring builders and thinkers working at the edges of the open web. The show combines the existing livestream office hours with in-depth conversations about the AT Protocol and broader open web initiatives.
